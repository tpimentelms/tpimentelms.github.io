Hey, I'm Tiago! I'm a Postdoc at ETH Zürich, working at the intersection of machine learning interpretability and psycholinguistics. My long-term goal is to understand how humans and machines process language. To this end, I take an interdisciplinary approach, drawing on information theory and causality to study the mechanisms behind both model behaviour and human cognition.
